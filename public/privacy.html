Privacy Policy

Your anonymity and data safety.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

Midnight Stories ("the Platform") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Content You Submit

When you submit a story or comment, we store the text content, any uploaded images, and a timestamp. We do not ask for or store your name, email, or any account credentials.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

For abuse prevention and legal compliance, we collect and store:

  • Hashed IP address: Your IP address is cryptographically hashed before storage. We never store your raw IP address. The hash is used solely for rate limiting and abuse investigation.
  • Timestamps: When content is submitted.
  • Browser cookies: Used for theme preference and crisis-banner dismissal state only. We do not use tracking cookies.

2.3 Uploaded Images

All metadata (EXIF data, GPS coordinates, device information) is automatically stripped from uploaded images before storage. Images are re-encoded server-side to ensure no hidden data persists.

3. How We Use Your Information

We believe in data minimization. We only collect and use the absolute minimum amount of information required to keep the Midnight Stories platform functional, safe, and welcoming for everyone. We do not use your information to build marketing profiles, track your behavior across other sites, or monetize your personal experiences.

  • Content display: To show approved stories and comments on the Platform.
  • Moderation: To screen content for safety and community guidelines compliance.
  • Abuse prevention: To enforce rate limits and ban abusive users.
  • Legal compliance: To respond to valid legal requests from authorities if required.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law (e.g., court orders, mandatory reporting obligations).
  • With content-safety scanning services that process images for safety checks (no personal data is shared with these services).

5. Data Retention

  • Published stories and comments: Retained until removed by a moderator or the author (via their submitter token).
  • Rejected/removed content: Deleted within 30 days of removal.
  • Hashed IP logs: Retained for up to 90 days, then automatically purged.
  • Uploaded images: Retained while the associated story is published; deleted when the story is removed.

6. Content Removal

If you submitted content using a submitter token, you can request deletion by contacting us with your token. Since the Platform is anonymous by design, we cannot verify authorship without the original token.

7. Cookies

We use minimal, functional cookies only:

  • Theme preference: Remembers your dark/light mode choice.
  • Banner state: Remembers if you dismissed the crisis resources banner.

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies that track individual users, or any third-party tracking technologies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold related to your submissions (requires your submitter token).
  • Request deletion of your submitted content.
  • Object to the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@midnightstories.example.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that a submission was made by someone under 18, we will remove it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the "last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@midnightstories.example.com
