How Support Tickets Work

Welcome to our Support Desk. Our team is here to help keep our community safe, clean, and friendly. When you send a report or question, we give you a unique ticket reference ID so you can track progress easily.

You can view updates on your tickets using the sidebar list on the left. Statuses show as Open (new report), Investigating (we are reviewing it), Waiting on User (we need details), or Resolved (completed).

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How fast will I get a response? Our support team reviews tickets daily. Most support tickets receive a response within 12 to 24 hours.
  • Can I reopen a resolved ticket? Yes! If your issue was marked resolved but you still need help, you can click Reopen Ticket within 7 days.
