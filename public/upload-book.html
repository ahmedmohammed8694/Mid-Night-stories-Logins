Publish & Self-Publish Books Online

Share educational materials, naval publications, and indie ebooks with our reader community.

Self-Publishing Author Guidelines & Moderation Standards

Midnight Stories invites independent authors, educators, and historians to self-publish digital books and reference manuals on our global reading platform. Whether you are publishing an educational manual, a historical reference, or creative literature, our open library gives your writing direct reach to thousands of engaged readers worldwide.

Document & Formatting Requirements

  • Supported Formats: We accept documents in .epub (recommended for responsive text reflow) and .pdf formats up to 50MB per file.
  • Metadata Accuracy: Every submission must contain a clear title, author or publisher name, target channel (Educational or Naval), and a descriptive category.
  • Cover Imagery: Include a high-resolution cover image in JPG, PNG, or WebP format (maximum file size 5MB) for optimal display in our digital library grid.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) for Self-Publishing Authors

How long does the administrative review process take?

Submissions are typically reviewed and approved within 24 to 48 hours. Once verified for safety and quality compliance, your book is immediately indexed across our digital library catalog.

Do I retain full copyright ownership of my self-published book?

Yes. Authors retain 100% of copyright ownership over their uploaded content. By uploading to Midnight Stories, you grant us a non-exclusive license to host and render your book in our online Reader Mode.

How do readers access and read self-published books on the platform?

Approved publications are accessible via our web-based Reader Mode, allowing users to track reading progress, save bookmarks, adjust typography, and read in distraction-free dark mode.
