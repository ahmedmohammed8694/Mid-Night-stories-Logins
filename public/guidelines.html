Community Guidelines

Empathy, honesty, and respect.

Last updated: July 2026

Midnight Stories is a community built on empathy, honesty, and respect. These guidelines exist to keep this space safe for everyone. Every moderator enforces these guidelines — violations may result in content removal and access restrictions. Every story deserves to be heard, even the ones told in whispers.

✅ What's Welcome

  • Personal stories — your own experiences, memories, reflections, and feelings.
  • Supportive comments — empathy, encouragement, and shared understanding.
  • Honest, raw, imperfect narratives — you don't need to have it all figured out.
  • LGBTQ+ stories — personal stories representing LGBTQ+ experiences are explicitly welcomed, supported, and held to the same community safety guidelines.
  • Symbolic or illustrative images — artwork, landscapes, objects that represent your story.

🚫 What's Not Allowed

Identifying Information

  • Do not include full names, addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying details of real people (including yourself).
  • Do not post photos where individuals can be identified.
  • If you need to reference someone, use a pseudonym or general description.

Photos of Minors

  • No photos of anyone under 18 — under any circumstances, for any reason. This is a zero-tolerance policy.
  • If you need to describe events involving children, use text only — never images.

Harassment & Hate

  • No content targeting individuals or groups based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, disability, or any other characteristic.
  • No bullying, intimidation, threats, or doxxing.
  • Disagreement is fine; cruelty is not.

Harmful Content

  • Do not share content that promotes, glorifies, or provides instructions for self-harm, suicide, or violence.
  • You may share your experiences with these topics — the distinction is between telling your story and encouraging harm.

Illegal Content

  • No content depicting or promoting illegal activity, including child exploitation material (which triggers automatic reporting to authorities).

Spam & Misuse

  • No commercial content, advertisements, or promotions.
  • No repetitive submissions, bot activity, or attempts to game the system.

🚩 Reporting Content

Every story and comment has a Report button. If you see something that violates these guidelines — or that makes you feel unsafe — please report it. Reports go directly to our moderation team. You don't need to explain yourself in detail; even a general reason helps us act quickly.

If content receives multiple reports, it may be automatically hidden pending review.

⚖️ Moderation Process

  1. All submissions are screened by automated systems for personal information, toxicity, and crisis language.
  2. Flagged content and all new submissions are reviewed by human moderators.
  3. Moderators can approve, reject, or edit content. Rejected content is removed permanently.
  4. Users who repeatedly violate guidelines may have their access restricted via IP-based bans.

💛 Crisis Situations

If your submission contains language suggesting you may be in crisis, we will show you crisis resource information before publishing your story. This is not censorship — it's care. We want to make sure you have access to immediate help.

For emergency resources, visit our Crisis Resources page.

Questions?

If you have questions about these guidelines or a moderation decision, contact us at community@midnightstories.example.com.
